About Cameron Jennings

As an aspiring engineer Cameron Jennings values his ability to problem solve and stay positive when working with a tight deadline or on a difficult project. Jennings enjoys reading and playing games in his pass time. Additionally, Jennings enjoys spending holidays with friends and family. In the future after completing college he hopes to get a job related to computer engineering.

After high school Jennings intends to apply for a four year college. Jennings will major in computer hardware engineering and attempt to get his master’s degree in that field. He wishes to remain within his home state of California during his college years. Jennings’ desired colleges are San Luis Obispo’s CalPoly, UC Berkeley, and Fresno State’s Lyles College of Engineering.
