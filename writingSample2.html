A notable written piece by Cameron Jennings is “Perseverance Leads to Success”. Written during his sophomore year, Jennings discusses the relation between perseverance and success and argues that growth and success come as the fruits of hard work. Jennings pulls from three main sources during this essay which include works like Melanie Greenberg’s "Can a Devastating Shark Attack Really Lead to a Better Life?"
